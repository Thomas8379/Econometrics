\section*{S7.2(B)}
We test the null that the Winner series is white noise using the Ljung--Box test with $m=4$. 
The test statistic is $Q_{LB} = 10.503$ with $\chi^2_4$ reference distribution and 
$p$-value $= 0.033$. 
At the 5\% level, we 
\textbf{reject} the null of white noise.
